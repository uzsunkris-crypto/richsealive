Dashboard

At-a-glance metrics and shortcuts into the app.

The RichSea Dashboard is the central hub for users to interact with the RichSea ecosystem. It provides real-time data, actionable insights, and direct access to platform features, making it easy for users to manage their assets, participate in community activities, and monitor their progress.

Key Features

1. Live Token Data Price Tracking: Displays the current price of $RICHSEA in USD and other supported currencies. 24h Change: Shows percentage and absolute changes in token price over the last 24 hours. Market Cap & Volume: Real-time updates on market capitalization and trading volume. Contract Address: Easy access to the official smart contract for transparency and verification.

2. Portfolio Management Wallet Integration: Connect your wallet (e.g., MetaMask, Trust Wallet) to view your $RICHSEA holdings. Balance Overview: See your token balance, staked amount, and farming rewards. Transaction History: Track recent transactions, including transfers, staking, and farming activities.

3. Staking & Farming Stake Tokens: Direct interface to stake $RICHSEA and earn rewards. Farming Pools: View available farming pools, APY rates, and your current participation. Claim Rewards: Easily claim earned tokens from staking and farming.

4. Community & Governance Voting Portal: Participate in governance by voting on proposals and platform upgrades. Announcements: Stay updated with the latest news, contests, and community events. Leaderboard: See top contributors and earn reputation points for active participation.

5. Educational Resources Academy Access: Quick links to RichSea Academy for tutorials, guides, and FAQs. Contest Participation: Join ongoing contests directly from the dashboard.

6. Security & Transparency Audit Reports: Access smart contract audit results and security updates. Open Source Links: Direct links to GitHub and other repositories for code transparency. User Experience Responsive Design: Optimized for desktop and mobile devices. Intuitive Navigation: Simple menus and clear calls-to-action for all major features. Personalization: Dashboard adapts to your activity, showing relevant stats and shortcuts.
